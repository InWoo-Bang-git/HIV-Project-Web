Frequently Asked Questions

1. How Does the Test Function?

The HIV Self Test comprises a petite cartridge housing a specialized test strip. Utilizing a simple finger prick method, it requires only a minimal blood sample. Rest assured, your results will be available in a concise 15-minute window.

2. Can I Trust the Test's Accuracy?

Our HIV Self Test boasts impressive accuracy, having consistently identified 99.6% of both HIV negative and positive samples in rigorous lab tests. However, it's worth noting that, like other HIV tests, there's a three-month window period. This indicates that recent exposures to HIV, specifically within the last three months, might not be detected. If you suspect a recent exposure, it's crucial to consult a medical professional or visit a specialized clinic.

3. What Should I Do if I Get a Positive Result?

While the HIV Self Test is an exceptionally accurate initial screening tool, a positive result isn't a definitive diagnosis. Further confirmatory tests by a healthcare provider are essential. Should your self-test result be positive, please refer to our support links or the detailed care card included in the test kit for guidance. If a subsequent test confirms a positive result, discussing treatment options with a doctor is vital. With today's advanced treatments, HIV is a manageable condition, allowing individuals to lead full, healthy lives.

4. Is the Device Reusable?

The HIV Self Test is designed for a one-time use to ensure the highest level of accuracy. Once utilized, it should be properly disposed of.

5. What's the Correct Disposal Method?

After completing the test, place all components, including the box, into the provided discreet disposal bag. Once sealed, this bag can be discarded with regular household waste.

6. How Will My Test Kit Be Delivered?

We prioritize your privacy. All our deliveries are facilitated through the Australia Post Express Post service. The HIV Self Test will be sent in the standard Express Post satchel without any explicit content disclosure.

7. Is the Packaging Discreet?

Absolutely. The test kit is dispatched in an Unmarked Post Satchel, ensuring utmost discretion. There won't be any markings or labels indicating its contents.
